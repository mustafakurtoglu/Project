My Master's Thesis

Building Outlier Detection Framework by Using Automated Machine Learning Methods

In the rapidly evolving landscape of data analysis, machine learning techniques stand at the forefront, offering powerful tools for extracting insights from vast pools of data. Yet, the application of these methods often stumbles upon a significant hurdle: the intensive manual effort required for data refinement and parameter optimization. This challenge is particularly pronounced in the realm of outlier detectionâ€”a critical process for identifying anomalies within data sets that, if overlooked, can lead to inaccurate analyses and misguided conclusions. My thesis, titled "Building Outlier Detection Framework by Using Automated Machine Learning Methods," addresses this crucial gap by proposing an advanced framework designed to streamline and automate the outlier detection process across various data types.

The cornerstone of my research is the development of an adaptable methodology capable of automating the identification of outliers within a myriad of dataset structures. This approach begins with a comprehensive framework tailored to recognize and categorize different types of data, followed by specialized preprocessing techniques aimed at optimizing data quality for each category. Such preparatory steps are crucial for setting the stage for effective outlier detection.

Integrating sophisticated outlier detection mechanisms, my method leverages extensive experimentation across diverse datasets to evaluate its efficacy. The goal is to achieve a high degree of precision and reliability in detecting anomalies automatically, thereby minimizing the need for manual intervention and significantly enhancing the efficiency of the detection process

Through a thorough evaluation across different datasets, this thesis aims to showcase the adaptability and consistent performance of the proposed framework, underscoring its potential to revolutionize outlier detection. By automating this critical process, the framework sets the stage for more sophisticated, accurate, and efficient data analysis techniques, marking a significant advancement in the field of machine learning and data science.

In conclusion, "Building Outlier Detection Framework by Using Automated Machine Learning Methods" presents a groundbreaking step towards simplifying and enhancing the process of anomaly detection. By harnessing the power of automated machine learning, this research paves the way for significant improvements in data analysis methodologies, promising a future where data-driven insights are more accessible and reliable than ever before.
